How to use:

You can easily use this mod by downloading Toolbox for Minecraft: Pocket Edition from Google PlayStore. Once you have installed the application follow this steps: Open the App. Click on "Launcher Options". Click on "ModPE's". Click on "+". And choose your sdcard if you have a internal storage, else extsdcard or microsd. Goto Download and find the file: "SnowCraftHax v?.? by TheBigHacker0100" ? is instead of the version numbers! Have fun!! Hope it works, please don't be crazy if there's not so much hacks or if it's buggy, IT'S STILL BETA!!!!
